History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small local meetup for developers. Over the years, it has grown into a global event attracting thousands of participants.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge-sharing in the tech community. TechCon empowers developers, researchers, and tech leaders to shape the future together.
